A Little Bit About Me

Patrick Muller

I was born in Colmar, France and spent all my childhood there. As a teenager I was always passionate with computing, coding and technologies. I was also an athlete and loved sports and I decided to go to college in Strasbourg, France to earn a Master's Degree in Exercise Science.
Shortly after graduating I decided to move to the United States to work as a Strength and Conditioning Coach. I have been very fortunate in my career and I spend the past 13 years in Ojai, California as the Fitness Directory for the Weil Tennis Academy. This is an amazing place but I feel that I achieved most of my goals in sports and fitness as a coach and I am ready for a new challenge.

I decided to go back to my other passion which is new technologies and coding. I am very passionate about it and I have been learning a lot of new skills in the past couple years. I love taking Bootcamps to learn new languages and technologies. I am eager to learn more and to start using my technical skills in the real world.
I am a very simple person, I love sports, the outdoors and traveling. The nature has so much to offer and I enjoy hiking and mountain biking. I was lucky to travel quiet a bit in my life in places like France, USA, Italy, Spain, Greece, Switzerland, Austria, Denmark, Mexico, Panama and Argentina but one of my dream and goal is someday to go around the world starting with Australia.
